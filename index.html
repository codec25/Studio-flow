Terms & Privacy

1. Cancellation Policy

Lessons cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice will not be credited back to your account. This policy ensures our coaches can maintain a stable schedule.

2. Refunds & Credits

Credit packs are non-refundable but are fully transferable to friends or family members. All credits expire 6 months from the date of purchase.

3. Your Privacy

We believe in data sovereignty. Your personal lesson data and history are stored locally on your device. We do not sell or track your personal information for third-party advertising.
